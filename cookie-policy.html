This website ("Site") uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to understand how visitors interact with the site. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions.

2. How We Use Cookies

We only use cookies through Google Analytics, which helps us understand how visitors engage with our website. These cookies collect anonymous data such as:

  • Number of visitors
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • Device type and browser used

This information helps us improve the design and content of the website.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics sets its own cookies to gather usage data. Google may use this data according to their own Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Your Choices

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on the website.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
